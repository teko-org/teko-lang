# Support

Teko is pre-release software under active development. Here's where to go for help:

- **Questions / ideas** — open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/schivei/teko-lang/discussions).
- **Bugs** — open an [issue](https://github.com/schivei/teko-lang/issues/new/choose) with a minimal `.tks` reproducer (the bug-report template shows what to include).
- **Building the toolchain** — see [docs/BUILDING.md](docs/BUILDING.md); if the steps there fail on your platform, that's a bug — report it.
- **Security issues** — never in public; see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

There is no commercial support and no SLA. Being a one-maintainer project, response times vary.
